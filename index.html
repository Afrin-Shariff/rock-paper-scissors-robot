ROCK - PAPER - SCISSORS

THERE ARE 3 MODES TO THIS EXCEPTIONAL ROCK PAPER SCISSORS GAME. THE FIRST IS EASY. THE SECOND IS MEDIUM. THE 3RD IS HARD. ONCE YOU READ ABOUT ALL OF THEM THEN CHOOSE YOUR LEVEL AND TRY TO BEAT THE COMPUTER. I WISH YOU ALL THE BEST WITH WHICHEVER LEVEL YOU CHOOSE EVEN IF IT IS THE EASY ONE!!! :)

EASY:

In this easy mode, it is very simple. It is just an ordinary game of rock paper scissors. The computer will get to choose randomly and it will try to beat you. First to 5 wins. If you beat the computer in 1 round then you get 1 point. If the computer beats you in one round then the computer gets 1 point. If you tie, then no one gets anything. GOOD LUCK!

MEDIUM:

In the middle mode, there are 5 options so you have to think harder. The computer still picks randomly but remember 5 is bigger than 3 so in this case it is harder.
